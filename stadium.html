Description

The Unipol Domus is the home stadium of Cagliari Calcio.
It is located in Cagliari, Sardinia, Italy.
The stadium was opened in 2017.

Interesting Facts

  • It is the temporary stadium of Cagliari Calcio.
  • It was built near the old Sant'Elia Stadium.
  • It hosts Serie A matches.
  • It has a modern and compact structure.
